## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no account, no catch. Run the
audit and get your results immediately. We ask for your
email after showing you the results — never before.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price traces to an official vendor pricing page,
verified during submission week. We show the verification
date in our pricing sources file.

**Q: Does SpendLens store my spending data?**
A: Your tool spend data is only saved when you request a
shareable link or enter your email. The audit itself runs
entirely in your browser.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits from
companies that overforecast or pivoted. If your audit shows
significant savings, Credex can help you capture even more
through discounted credits at the same quality.

**Q: Who is this built for?**
A: Startup founders, CTOs, and engineering managers at
companies with 1 to 200 people who pay for at least one
AI tool and want to know if they are spending wisely.

## Tone Notes

- Direct and honest — no hype
- Numbers over adjectives — "$340/mo saved" not "massive savings"
- Respect the user's time — they're busy, get to the point
- No dark patterns — email asked after value shown, never before
