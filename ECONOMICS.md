# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Here is my estimate with reasoning shown:

A startup spending $500/mo on AI tools is a reasonable target customer. If Credex saves them 20% through discounted credits that's $100/mo in savings for the customer, which means they'd pay somewhere between $400 and $480/mo through Credex instead of $500 direct.

Credex margin on that: roughly 15 to 25% depending on how the credits were sourced. Call it 20% — so $80 to $100/mo gross profit per customer.

Customer lifetime: startups that find a working vendor tend to stick for at least 12 months.

**LTV per customer: roughly $1,000 to $1,200**

That's a conservative estimate. A larger customer spending $2,000/mo is worth 4x more.

## What Gets This to $1M ARR in 18 Months

Monthly recurring revenue target: $56,000/mo by month 18.

At $100/mo gross profit per customer that's 560 active customers.

To get 560 customers by month 18 you need roughly 35 new customers per month by month 12 (accounting for ramp time).

At 8 customers per 1,000 visitors you need 4,375 visitors per month, sustained.

That requires either:

- The shareable URL going viral — if 30% of users share their audit and each share brings 3 new visitors, the tool grows 0.9x per cohort. Not quite viral but close enough with top-of-funnel content.
- One paid channel at under $100 CAC — Google Ads on "AI tool cost" or "cursor vs copilot" at $2 CPC with 5% audit completion is $40 CAC. That works at this LTV.
- Credex in-product distribution to existing customers — this is the fastest path because it's zero CAC and the audience is already qualified.

The honest answer is that $1M ARR in 18 months requires the in-product Credex channel to work. Organic alone gets you to $200k to $400k ARR in that window.
